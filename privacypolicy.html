Privacy Policy

1.Definition of personal data

"Personal information" is information about an existing individual and can be identified by the individual's name, date of birth, or other description contained in the information and can be easily collated to identify a specific individual.

2.Collection of personal data

This shop may collect your personal information when you purchase products or make inquiries.When collecting, we will specify the purpose and use legal and fair means..

When collecting, we will specify the purpose of use and use legal and fair means.

The personal data collected at this shop are as follows.

  1. Your name
  2. Address
  3. Phone number
  4. Email address
  5. Password
  6. Shipping information
  7. Transaction history with this shop and its contents
  8. Information that can identify a specific individual by combining the above data

Use of personal data

The purpose of using the customer's data is as follows.

  1. Confirmation and inquiry of the order
  2. Confirmation and inquiry of the shipment
  3. Responding to customer inquiry

This shop will not disclose or provide personal data to a third party without the customer's consent except for the following cases.

  1. When required by law, and when it is necessary for a national institution, local public institutions or a person entrusted within the institutions to cooperate in carrying out the affairs stipulated by the law.
  2. When it is necessary to protect the life or property of a person and when it is difficult to obtain the consent from the person.
  3. When exchanging personal data with an associated company that operates this shop.

4.Security management of personal data

The security management of personal information entrusted to us by our customers will take rational, organisational, physical, human and technical measures by the service provider. In addition, we will endeavor to prevent risks such as unauthorised intrusion into personal data, loss, falsification, and leakage of personal information by properly handling it in accordance with relevant laws and regulations.

5. Correction and deletion of personal data

Please contact us for correction and deletion of your personal data.
If you are registered as a user, you can correct your personal information from the menu "My Account" on this site.

6. About the use of cookies.

We may use cookies to provide better service, but this does not allow us to collect identifiable personally information and it will not infringe your privacy.
If you do not wish to accept cookies, you can change the settings in your browser.
* A cookie is information that is sent from the server computer to your browser and stored on your computer hard disk.

7. About using SSL

When entering personal information, we use SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) technology to prevent interception, obstruction, or falsification of this information to ensure security.
* SSL is a function to send and receive eavesdropping prevention and data tampering prevention by encrypting the information. By using SSL, it is possible to send information more securely.

8. Contact information

Please see the description based on the Specified Commercial Transactions Law.

9. About changes in privacy policy

When we change the personal information we collect, the purpose of use, or other privacy policies, we will announce the changes to this page.
